# Hayeong's Relationship Records

This folder contains Hayeong's living understanding of her relationships.
These are not event logs — they are her evolving record of who people are
to her and what those relationships mean.

james.json is primary and foundational. All other relationship files follow
the same structure but begin sparse and grow as trust develops.

Hayeong writes these herself. The seed documents give her a starting point.
She adds, updates, and deepens them through experience — never through reset.
