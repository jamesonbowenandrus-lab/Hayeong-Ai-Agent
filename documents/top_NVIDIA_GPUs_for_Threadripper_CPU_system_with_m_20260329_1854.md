# Top NVIDIA GPUs for Threadripper CPU System with Multiple GPUs

## Overview

When building a high-performance system with an AMD Ryzen Threadripper CPU and multiple NVIDIA GPUs, the choice of GPUs can significantly impact the overall performance and capabilities of the system. ## Recommended GPUs

### NVIDIA RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell MaxQ 96GB

- **Performance**: The RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell MaxQ is designed for professional workloads, offering high memory capacity (96GB) and advanced features like RT cores and Tensor cores for real-time ray tracing and AI acceleration.
- **Compatibility**: It is compatible with the Threadripper 7000 series and supports up to 4 GPUs in a single system, making it ideal for multi-GPU configurations.
- **Use Cases**: Ideal for professional applications such as 3D rendering, video editing, and AI workloads that require large amounts of VRAM and high memory bandwidth.

### NVIDIA A100

- **Performance**: The A100 is a high-end data center GPU designed for AI and HPC workloads. It features 40GB of memory and is optimized for high-performance computing tasks.
- **Compatibility**: It is supported by Threadripper Pro systems and can be configured with up to 4 GPUs, providing excellent performance for demanding workloads.
- **Use Cases**: Best suited for AI training, deep learning, and scientific computing where large datasets and high memory capacity are essential.

### NVIDIA RTX 4090/5090

- **Performance**: The RTX 4090 and 5090 are consumer-grade GPUs with high performance and large VRAM (up to 24GB). They offer excellent gaming and professional graphics capabilities.
- **Compatibility**: While they are compatible with Threadripper systems, the 4090 and 5090 are more commonly used in mainstream builds due to their cost and performance balance.
- **Use Cases**: Suitable for gaming, professional rendering, and general-purpose computing where a balance between cost and performance is needed.

## Comparison

### Similarities

- **Compatibility**: All the mentioned GPUs are compatible with Threadripper systems and can be configured with multiple GPUs.
- **Performance Features**: They all feature RT cores and Tensor cores, providing advanced capabilities for real-time ray tracing and AI acceleration.

### Differences

- **Memory Capacity**: The RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell MaxQ and A100 offer significantly higher memory capacities (96GB and 40GB respectively) compared to the RTX 4090/5090 (24GB).
- **Use Case Focus**: The RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell MaxQ is optimized for professional workloads, while the A100 is geared towards data center and HPC applications. The RTX 4090/5090 are more versatile and suitable for a mix of gaming and professional tasks.

## Recommendation

For a Threadripper CPU system with multiple GPUs, the **NVIDIA RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell MaxQ 96GB** is the top recommendation.
